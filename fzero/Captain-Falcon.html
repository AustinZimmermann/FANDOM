Captain Falcon

“I’m Captain Falcon! The galaxy’s most famous bounty hunter and F-Zero pilot!”

Captain Falcon (キャプテン・ファルコン, Kyaputen Farukon) is the main protagonist of the F-Zero series. He is a mysterious bounty hunter and one of the greatest F-Zero racers in the galaxy. He is the owner of the Blue Falcon, one of the most balanced machines in the Grand Prix.

Characteristics

Falcon is often seen as a hero with a sense of justice, though he is primarily motivated by the thrill of the race and the pursuit of dangerous criminals. He lives on a series of islands off the coast of Port Town, where he spends most of his time in isolation when not racing or hunting.

F-Zero Racing

He has been a staple in the F-Zero Grand Prix since its inception. His racing style is known for being aggressive yet technical. Many fans believe he is the only pilot capable of pushing the Blue Falcon to its true limits.

Did you know?

Captain Falcon’s real name is rumored to be Douglas Jay Falcon, though this is debated among fans and depends on the specific timeline (Game vs Anime).
