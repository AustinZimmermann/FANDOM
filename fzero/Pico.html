Pico

Pico (ピコ, Piko) is a recurring character in the F-Zero series. He is a former member of the Porpohian army on his home planet, Death Wind. Pico is known for his incredibly aggressive driving style and his signature machine, the Wild Goose.

Pico is an alien from the planet Death Wind. He has a very aggressive personality and is known to be quite reckless on the track. His history as a soldier has made him a disciplined, yet dangerous competitor who isn’t afraid to push other racers off the track to secure a victory.

Characteristics

Pico’s appearance is distinct, characterized by his green skin, sharp teeth, and humanoid yet reptilian features. He wears a specialized combat suit designed for high-speed racing and environmental protection.

Racing Style

Pico’s racing style is defined by his willingness to engage in physical combat with other machines. The Wild Goose is specifically built to withstand heavy impacts, making Pico a formidable opponent in the “Elimination” or “Death Race” modes of various F-Zero titles.

“I’ll crush anyone who gets in my way! The Wild Goose is built for one thing: Destruction!” — Pico (F-Zero GX)
