Dr. Stewart

Dr. Stewart (ドクター スチュワート, Dokutā Suchuwāto) is a recurring character in the F-Zero series. He is a high-class surgeon who has participated in the Grand Prix since the original F-Zero game for the SNES.

Biography

Dr. Robert Stewart was once a top-tier surgeon who lived a comfortable life. However, following the death of his father, Kevin Stewart, he inherited both a vast fortune and the legendary racing machine, the Golden Fox.

Unlike many other pilots who race for fame or fortune, Dr. Stewart views the F-Zero Grand Prix as a way to honor his father’s memory and to prove the capabilities of the machine he left behind. He is known for his calm, analytical approach to racing, often using his medical knowledge to understand the physiological limits of a pilot during high-speed maneuvers.

Golden Fox

The Golden Fox is machine #03 in the F-Zero lineup. It is famous for its exceptional acceleration and boost capabilities, though it lacks the high top speed and body strength of machines like the Blue Falcon or Fire Stingray.

Trivia

  • In the F-Zero: GP Legend anime, he is portrayed as a member of the High Stakes racing team.
  • His design has remained remarkably consistent across all games, featuring his signature yellow scarf and sunglasses.
  • He is considered one of the "Original Four" pilots, alongside Captain Falcon, Samurai Goroh, and Pico.
