Star Wars: Episode III – Revenge of the Sith (2005)

From DVD Wiki, the encyclopedia of home video releases

Star Wars: Episode III – Revenge of the Sith is the 2005 DVD release of the final installment in the Star Wars prequel trilogy. Released in the United States on November 1, 2005, the DVD was highly anticipated for its digital-to-digital transfer and extensive bonus features.

Synopsis

Three years into the Clone Wars, the Jedi Knights lead a vast clone army into a galaxy-wide battle against the Separatists. When the sinister Sith unveil a thousand-year-old plot to rule the galaxy, the Republic crumbles and from its ashes rises the evil Galactic Empire. Jedi hero Anakin Skywalker is seduced by the dark side of the Force to become the Emperor’s new apprentice – Darth Vader. The Jedi are decimated, as Obi-Wan Kenobi and Jedi Master Yoda are forced into hiding.
