Captain Falcon

Edit Talk Share ...

Captain Falcon (キャプテン ファルコン, Kyaputen Farukon) is the main protagonist of the F-Zero series. He is a mysterious bounty hunter who is one of the best racers in the galaxy. Besides his racing career, little is known about his past, other than the fact that he is from Port Town.

Falcon is the pilot of the Blue Falcon, and his racing number is 07. He is a well-rounded racer, and his machine is known for being balanced in all aspects.

Captain Falcon is also a playable character in the Super Smash Bros. series, where he has become widely known for his “Falcon Punch” and “Falcon Kick” attacks.

Characteristics

Captain Falcon is a tall, muscular man with a build fitting for a bounty hunter. His exact appearance is unknown, as he is always seen wearing his signature racing helmet with a red visor that obscures his eyes. He wears a blue racing suit with yellow gloves and boots, and a red scarf. In F-Zero GX, his outfit is more detailed and armored.

Biography

Background

Little is known about Captain Falcon’s past. It is rumored that he was once an officer in the Internova Police Force, but left to become a bounty hunter. He goes by the title “Captain” from his time in the force. He is a native of Port Town.
