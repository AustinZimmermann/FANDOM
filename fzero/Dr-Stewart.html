Dr. Stewart

“I’m a doctor first, a racer second.”

Dr. Stewart (ドクター スチュワート, Dokutā Suchuwāto), better known as Robert Stewart, is a major character and one of the original four pilots in the F-Zero series. He is a world-class surgeon who took up F-Zero racing after his father’s death, utilizing his surgical precision to handle high-speed maneuvers.

Biography

Dr. Stewart was a long-time surgeon whose surgical skills were considered legendary. After the death of his father, Kevin Stewart, Robert decided to participate in the F-Zero Grand Prix. Unlike many other racers who are motivated by fame or money, Stewart often races to fund his medical research or to provide aid to those in need.

His calm demeanor and focus under pressure make him a formidable opponent. He is known for his signature golden suit and yellow scarf, reflecting his aristocratic and refined personality.

Machine: Golden Fox

The Golden Fox is one of the oldest machines in the F-Zero circuit. It was originally designed by his father. It is famous for its exceptional acceleration and recovery capabilities, though it lacks the high top speed and durability of heavier machines like the Blue Falcon or Fire Stingray.

Trivia

  • In the F-Zero: GP Legend anime, he is a member of the High Stakes racing team.
  • He is the only racer to have appeared in every single F-Zero game to date.
  • His design was inspired by 1970s disco icons and classic refined gentlemen.
