Samurai Goroh

“I’ll show you who’s the best racer in the universe! Captain Falcon is mine!”

Samurai Goroh (サムライ ゴロー, Samurai Gorō) is a recurring character in the F-Zero series. He is a rival to Captain Falcon and a bounty hunter, as well as the leader of a notorious group of space bandits. Despite his villainous associations, he is often portrayed as a boisterous, somewhat comical character who is obsessed with defeating Falcon on and off the racetrack.

Biography

Goroh has appeared in every F-Zero game to date. He pilots the Fire Stingray, a machine known for its incredibly high top speed and heavy weight, making it a formidable presence in the Grand Prix. His main base of operations is usually cited as the Red Canyon, where his gang resides.

In F-Zero GX, Goroh’s motivation is centered around his intense jealousy of Captain Falcon’s success and fame. He often attempts to sabotage Falcon or claim bounties before him, usually with loud declarations of his own superiority.

Appearance

Goroh is easily recognizable by his traditional Japanese-inspired attire, including a modified kimono, a katana (which he often brandishes during cutscenes), and his signature kabuki-style makeup and wild hair.

F-Zero Grand Prix

As a racer, Goroh is one of the “Original Four” (alongside Captain Falcon, Dr. Stewart, and Pico). His machine, the Fire Stingray, has the following stats in most iterations:

  • Body: A
  • Boost: D
  • Grip: B
