Abby Hatcher (or simply Abby) is the main protagonist of the series of the same name. She is a 7-year-old girl who is one of the only few humans who knows of the existence of Fuzzlies and the only one who can understand their language. Together with her best friend Bozzly, she goes on adventures to help Fuzzlies in need.

Physical appearance

Abby has light skin, purple eyes, and dark magenta hair pulled into a high ponytail with lighter pink highlights. She wears a pink t-shirt with a purple heart on it, a purple skirt, pink leggings, and purple and pink sneakers. She also wears a pair of Fuzzly-spotter glasses that she uses to find Fuzzlies.

Personality

Abby is a kind, caring, and adventurous girl who loves to help others, especially Fuzzlies. She is very optimistic and always sees the best in everyone. She is also very resourceful and uses her Fuzzly-spotter glasses to solve problems.
